# Dressi 👔✨

**Dressi: Outfit Inspo & Shop AI App**

Dressi is a fashion discovery, creator, and shopping platform that helps users find complete outfits, personalize their style, follow fashion creators, and shop entire looks through a seamless experience.

Instead of searching for individual clothing items, Dressi focuses on complete outfit inspiration. Users can browse styles such as Old Money, Streetwear, Minimalist, Business Casual, Y2K, Vacation, and more, then save, customize, and purchase entire outfits.

---

## Mission

Help users discover their personal style and purchase complete outfits with minimal friction.

Dressi combines:

* Fashion inspiration
* Creator content
* AI-powered outfit recommendations
* Social discovery
* Unified shopping experience
